Break Buddies

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Break Buddies ("we", "us", "the App") helps people nearby connect for a quick, casual break — chai, coffee, a walk, or just a chat. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it's handled.

What we collect

  • Name and avatar — the display name and emoji avatar you choose at sign-up. We do not require your real legal name, email, phone number, or any government ID.
  • Approximate location — used only to show you breaks happening nearby and to show your break to people nearby. Location is used live, while you use the app, and is not stored as a permanent history.
  • Break details you create — the activity type, meeting spot, and duration you choose when you start a break.
  • Chat messages — messages sent between two people who have matched on a break, so you can coordinate meeting up.
  • Push notification subscription — only if you turn notifications on, so we can alert you about join requests or replies. You can turn this off any time.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • We do not track you outside the app.
  • We do not collect precise GPS history or store where you've been over time.
  • We do not require payment information — Break Buddies does not process payments.
A note on break types. Break Buddies lets users label their own break with a simple, informal tag — chai, coffee, a walk, drinks, or similar everyday activities — purely so nearby people know what kind of break is happening. Break Buddies does not promote, advertise, sell, or encourage the use of tobacco, cigarettes, or any smoking product. Any such label is optional, user-generated, and treated the same as any other casual activity tag on the platform. We do not run or accept advertising related to tobacco or smoking products of any kind.

Who can see your information

Your name and avatar are visible to other users near you when you have an active break, or when you view breaks near you. Your exact location is never shown to another user — only an approximate distance and a meeting spot you choose to share. Chat messages are only visible to the two people in that conversation.

How long we keep data

Break details expire automatically after your chosen duration. Your account (name, avatar) is kept until you ask us to delete it.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will remove your information within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

Break Buddies is not intended for children. Users should be at least 18 years old.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at hello@breakbuddies.in.
